Tribute to A.P.J Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J Abdul Kalam

A.P.J Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India and was renowned scientist,known for his work in India's space and missile programs

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Led India's succesful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of fire" and "India 2020"
  • Recieved the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
